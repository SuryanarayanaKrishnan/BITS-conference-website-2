Submission



To participate in the InterSys2023, the authors may submit papers (10-12 pages, including References) or abstracts (300-500 words), in English.



  • ● Papers must be prepared using new CEUR-style template .

  • ● Abstracts are not intended for publication (presentation only).

  • ● All submissions to the InterSys2023 workshop must be done via the EasyChair system .

  • ● To be accepted to the workshop, a paper/abstract must be original, not published anywhere else before and not being under review.

  • ● The authors of accepted papers and abstracts will be invited to present their work in the workshop.

  • ● The registration fee is to be paid to IMS-2023 and allows participation in the main conference in either online or face-to-face format (including all the social events). For the authors from the participating universities, the fee will be waived.
