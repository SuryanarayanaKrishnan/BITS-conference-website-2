About Us

International Workshop Interactive Systems & Information Society Technologies (InterSys2023) is a joint event held by the Federal University of Paraná (UFPR, Curitiba, Brazil), BITS Pilani (Dubai Campus) and ITMO University (St. Petersburg, Russia) on June 26-28, 2023 as a part of the International Conference Internet and Modern Society (IMS-2023). The event is supported by the Journal of Interactive Systems (JIS, Brazil) and the International Journal of Open Information Technologies (INJOIT, Russia). The goal of the InterSys2023 workshop is to discuss to the problems of interactive technologies development in the digital environment (science, education, healthcare, business, public administration and civil society).

Key Topics of the workshop are the following:

Human Computer Interaction in Education & Science




Information Society Technologies: Government - Business - Citizens

Complexity in Human-Computer Interfaces

Human Values in Digital Environment

Interactive Systems in Digital Health



Computational Thinking for Digital Culture Development




Methodology Based on Computer Vision and Machine Learning
